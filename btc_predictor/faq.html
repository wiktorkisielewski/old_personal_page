What should I do?

Predict what the Bitcoin price ($USD/2 decimals) will be on 1 JANUARY 2019[00:00;01]
and submit it with your BTC address, then wait for your reward.
The contest will last 72 hours before the countdown ends.

What exchange will be used to compare predicted prices?

Coinbase

What happens when no prediction will be correct?

The cumulated prize will be added to the next contest.

What if more than one predictions will be accurate?

The prize will be equally split for every winner.

Where can I check the results?

https://twitter.com/bitpredictor

How is the prize paid?

It is automatically sent to BTC address submitted with correct prediction.

Do you accept SegWit addresses?

Of course, we do.

How many times can I submit prediction?

There is no limit for submissions correlated with one address.
